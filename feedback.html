Climate Action Programmes in Nepal

Nepalese Youth for Climate Action

A student-led organisation campaigning for ambitious climate policy in Nepal. Run workshops, write to elected representatives, and organise campus events to build momentum for change.

Get Involved

Kathmandu Swap & Share

A campus-based clothes and goods swap programme reducing fast fashion waste in Kathmandu. Attend termly swap events or volunteer as an organiser to earn green credits.

Join Now

Nepal Community Farming Network

Volunteer at organic farming spaces across Nepal's valleys. Learn sustainable agriculture, improve local food security and reduce food miles across Terai and hill communities.

Learn More

Solar Together Nepal

A group-buying scheme for solar panels and battery storage tailored for Nepali households. Join thousands of families collectively purchasing renewable energy installations at reduced cost.
